## Background

Initial analysis (see `files/initial_analysis.md`) mapped existing research capabilities against all 9 CFP topic areas. Key findings:

### Alignment Strength by Topic

| CFP Topic | Fit Level | Key Overlaps |
|-----------|-----------|--------------|
| Knowledge Grounding | Extremely Strong | Agentic KG-RAG, multi-source fusion, provenance chains |
| Agentic AI | Extremely Strong | Multi-agent orchestration, validation agents, agent governance |
| Agentic Evaluation | Strong | Validation matrix frameworks, provenance-backed evaluation |
| AI for Science/Engineering | Very Strong | Construction-AI, digital twins, PDE simulation |
| Reasoning | Strong | Engineering reasoning, temporal/spatial, scientific graph constraints |
| Efficient GenAI | Moderate-Strong | KG pruning reduces token overhead, structured retrieval efficiency |
| Responsible AI | Moderate | Provenance + evidence chains, human-in-the-loop validation |
| Personalization | Not analyzed | — |
| Foundation Model Improvements | Not analyzed | — |

### Three Candidate Directions

**Rank 1: Provenance-Aware Agentic KG Systems**
- Primary: Knowledge Grounding / Secondary: Agentic AI
- Highest funding probability, strongest differentiation from generic RAG proposals
- Direct match with CFP language, Amazon enterprise relevance

**Rank 2: Structured Validation Framework for KG-Grounded Agents**
- Primary: Agentic Evaluation / Secondary: Responsible AI
- Safest, most publishable, high strategic value to Amazon
- Leverages existing validation matrix research

**Rank 3: AI-Accelerated Engineering via Agentic Digital Twins**
- Primary: AI for Science/Engineering / Secondary: Agentic AI
- Most visionary, strongest narrative
- Higher risk, requires strong engineering framing

## Open Questions for Deeper Analysis

1. **Competitive landscape**: What are other VT labs likely to propose? Overlap risk?
2. **Amazon strategic priorities**: Which topics does Amazon care most about *this year*?
3. **Novelty depth**: For Rank 1, how does PA-AKG differ from existing KG-RAG systems (e.g., GraphRAG, KGQA)? Need concrete differentiation.
4. **Feasibility**: Can any of these produce meaningful results in 1 year with doctoral fellowship resources?
5. **Combinability**: Can elements of Rank 2 (evaluation) be folded into Rank 1 as a deliverable?
6. **PI alignment**: Which direction best matches the PI's existing publication record and funded work?
7. **Amazon-specific use cases**: Which proposal best maps to Amazon product/service areas (Alexa, AWS, retail, logistics)?
