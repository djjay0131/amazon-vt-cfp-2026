# Amazon-VT CFP 2026-2027: AI Research Proposal

## Project Overview

Research proposal for the **Amazon-Virginia Tech Initiative 2026-2027 Call for Proposals**. This initiative bridges academic and industry research in artificial intelligence, funding research projects and doctoral fellowships.

## CFP Topics of Interest

Proposals must advance research in one or more of:

- **Foundation Model Improvements** — architectures, training, multi-modal, continual learning, RL
- **Foundation Model/Agentic Evaluation** — benchmarks, robust evaluation
- **Efficient Generative AI & Inference** — cloud/on-device, long context, inference-time scaling
- **Reasoning** — commonsense, domain-specific, temporal/spatial, planning
- **Knowledge Grounding** — multi-modal grounding, memory-augmented AI, retrieval, knowledge fusion
- **Agentic AI** — autonomous systems, multi-agent, orchestration, agent-environment simulation
- **Personalization** — system-level, dialog, prompt-based, personalized RAG
- **Responsible Generative AI** — red teaming, robustness, guardrails, frontier risks
- **AI Accelerated Science & Engineering** — GenAI for physics, math, chemistry, biology, engineering, etc.
