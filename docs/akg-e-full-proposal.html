Methodology Highlights

The proposal builds the AKG-E framework around three reusable layers (domain ontology, simulation integration, VVUQ validation) and an explicit agentic propose → VVUQ → feedback loop — the loop is the net-new contribution beyond existing KG+agent systems. The differentiator from text-based critic loops (Reflexion, Self-RAG) is that AKG-E’s gate closes against quantitative simulation outputs held to codified engineering standards (AWC-NDS / HCM2022 / NERC-TPL), enabling iterative validation at simulation-loop speed where manufacturer-cycle verification today takes weeks to months.

Evaluation framework (three tiers, in order)

  • Correctness (non-negotiable floor) — constraint-violation rate vs codified standards. The abstract's >50% violation-reduction target is preserved as the floor.
  • Calibration — empirical coverage probability of the 95% CI against ground-truth simulator outputs.
  • Efficiency (headline contribution) — wall-clock time-to-validated-decision vs the manufacturer-cycle baseline.

Baselines (three-tier ablation)

  • B0 — Claude alone (LLM-only)
  • B1 — Claude + graph-RAG over curated KG
  • B2 — Claude + graph-RAG + agentic VVUQ loop (full AKG-E)

Domains

Two funded domains during the 3-month build: Construction (LVL VVUQ pipeline as anchor, BIM/IFC + FEA) and Transportation (existing VTTI digital-twin proposal as anchor, signal-timing scenarios). Energy is included as a designed extension (NERC-TPL contingency analysis) for Months 4–12.
